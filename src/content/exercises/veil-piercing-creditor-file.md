## Facts

A creditor contracted with East Bridge Trading Co. The purchase orders use East Bridge's seal, but sales calls came from West Bridge Machinery staff. Payments moved through a third affiliate, and all three companies share one office and one finance manager.

## Questions

1. Which facts support separate personality?
2. Which facts support confusion of personality or abuse of limited liability?
3. What additional evidence should a creditor seek?

## Hints

Look for assets, accounts, personnel, contracts, seals, public registration, websites, invoices, and actual performance.

## Discussion Guide

Students should build an evidence matrix rather than jump straight to veil piercing. Strong answers distinguish normal group coordination from legally significant confusion.
