## Use It For

Use this when a board, controller, or issuer may have failed to disclose material information accurately, completely, or promptly.

## Teaching Notes

For listed companies, governance problems often become disclosure problems before they become litigation problems. Use this 2025 order for current problems rather than the 2021 version.
