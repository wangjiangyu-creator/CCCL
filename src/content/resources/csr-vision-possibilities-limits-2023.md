## Brief English Introduction

Feng and Jia argue that CSR is difficult because it sits between efficiency and fairness and because Chinese company law remains cautious about non-market mechanisms. ESG disclosure is treated as a possible practical route.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether disclosure can make CSR meaningful without asking courts to define all stakeholder obligations directly.
