## Use It For

Use this when comparing directors' duties in solvent and insolvent zones, especially after students have seen Caremark and UK creditor-duty materials.

## Teaching Notes

The case shows that director-liability analysis can move from shareholder governance to creditor protection when financial distress becomes acute.
