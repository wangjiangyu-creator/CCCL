## Use It For

Use this after Salomon to test when separate personality can be disregarded and when ordinary private-law doctrines do the work instead.

## Teaching Notes

The case is a disciplined comparator for Chinese personality-denial doctrine because it narrows veil-piercing rather than treating it as a general fairness power.
