## Use It For

Use this case to test how far counterparties must investigate internal approval before relying on a legal representative's act in an external guarantee transaction.

## Teaching Notes

The case is a useful bridge between the older Company Law Article 16 debate, the Jiu Min Minutes, and the later Contract Book Interpretation approach to ultra vires representation.
