## Use It For

Use this with Hong Kong, Singapore, and Chinese minority-remedy materials to compare the line between hard bargaining and unfair shareholder treatment.

## Teaching Notes

The case is good for testing whether students can identify the source of the alleged expectation rather than assuming disappointment equals unfairness.
