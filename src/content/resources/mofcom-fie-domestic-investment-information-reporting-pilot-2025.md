## Use It For

Use this when a foreign-invested enterprise makes a downstream domestic investment and students need to ask whether the ordinary 2019 information-reporting system has been extended by a pilot rule.

## Teaching Notes

This is a good practical detail for transaction checklists. A downstream investment can look purely domestic at the company-law level while still carrying foreign-investment reporting consequences.
