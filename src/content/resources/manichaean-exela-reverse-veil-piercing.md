## Brief English Introduction

The court allowed an outsider reverse-piercing theory to proceed at the pleading stage because the alleged structure would otherwise leave a judgment creditor without an effective remedy.

## Use It For

Use this with the SPC FaDaWang Q&A and Singapore's reverse-piercing cases to compare how courts protect creditors without harming innocent shareholders or creditors of affiliated entities.

## Teaching Notes

The safeguards matter: reverse piercing is treated as rare, creditor-facing, and sensitive to third-party prejudice.
