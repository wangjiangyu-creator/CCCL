## Brief English Introduction

This case is the anchor authority for treating the legal representative's position as grounded in corporate authorization rather than a free-standing personal status. Once the competent company organ has removed the person under the articles, the representative authority ends internally and the company must take the public-registration steps needed to make the record truthful.

## Use It For

Use it to connect corporate personality with public registration: outsiders see the registered legal representative, but the internal source of authority remains the company's appointment.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students why the court can require a registration change without itself choosing the next legal representative.
