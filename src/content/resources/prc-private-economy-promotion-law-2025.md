## Use It For

Use this as the statutory anchor for private company protection, especially equal treatment, fair competition, government service obligations, property protection, procuratorial supervision, and government liability for unperformed policy commitments or contracts.

## Teaching Notes

The law does not replace the Company Law for private companies. It adds a public-law protection layer around ordinary private business organizations.
