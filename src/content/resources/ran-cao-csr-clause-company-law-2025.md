## Brief English Introduction

Ran and Cao argue that CSR should not remain an empty slogan. If a company writes social-responsibility purposes into its articles, managers may be held to those purposes through fiduciary-duty analysis.

## Teaching Notes

Use this to ask when CSR becomes law: through statute alone, through articles, through director duties, or through public-law enforcement.
