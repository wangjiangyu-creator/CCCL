## Brief English Introduction

The article focuses on the procedural side of legal representative expungement. It asks how courts should handle cases involving discredited, revoked, or bankrupt companies, where removal may protect the individual but also affect creditors, registration authorities, and successor representation.

## Use It For

Use it to connect the nominee-representative cases with civil procedure and market-entity registration.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students what the "best alternative" should mean when no successor legal representative is available.
