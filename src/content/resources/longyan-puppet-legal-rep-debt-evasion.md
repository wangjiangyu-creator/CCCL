## Brief English Introduction

The case treats a high-age nominal legal representative as evidence of a deeper debt-evasion structure. The real controllers allegedly transferred shares, installed a puppet representative, and failed to hand over seals and accounting records, leading the court to impose responsibility on the people who actually controlled the company.

## Use It For

Use it to connect legal representative doctrine with actual-controller responsibility and creditor protection.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether the decisive fact is the representative's age, the missing records, the controller's conduct, or the creditor harm.
