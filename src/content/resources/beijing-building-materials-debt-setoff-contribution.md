## Brief English Introduction

This case gives concrete conditions for treating a shareholder's claim against the company as contribution performance, including article amendment, solvency, and registration filing.

## Use It For

Use it with Article 48 of the 2023 Company Law and the SPC draft interpretation provisions on claim contributions and set-off.

## Teaching Notes

The case forces students to ask whether set-off is a neutral payment method or a disguised priority for insiders over external creditors.
