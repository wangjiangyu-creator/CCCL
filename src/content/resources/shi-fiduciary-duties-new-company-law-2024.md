## Brief English Introduction

Shi reads the revised Company Law as a conscious restructuring of Chinese fiduciary-duty doctrine rather than a simple transplant of common-law concepts.

## Use It For

Use this as a doctrinal map for Articles 180 to 192, especially when students need to connect loyalty, diligence, conflict approval, and controller responsibility.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students which parts of the statute impose conduct rules and which parts create remedial hooks. The article helps them see why that distinction matters.
