## Use It For

Use this with Waddington and Singapore oppression cases to ask when a shareholder remedy is truly personal and when the claim belongs to the company.

## Teaching Notes

The case forces students to identify the right claimant and the right remedy before debating whether the alleged conduct is unfair.
