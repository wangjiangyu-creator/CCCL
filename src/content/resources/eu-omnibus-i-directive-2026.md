## Use It For

Use this as the current-law update to the 2025 Omnibus discussion. It helps students avoid treating the February 2025 proposal as the last word.

## Teaching Notes

For class discussion, ask whether simplification represents retreat from ESG, better tailoring of ESG, or a shift from broad coverage to high-impact companies.
