## Brief English Introduction

This article traces China's movement from strict statutory capital to subscription reform and then toward limited authorized-capital mechanisms. It helps students place the 2023 revision inside a longer capital-system trajectory.

## Use It For

Use it to compare legal-capital architecture across reform periods.

## Teaching Notes

The article predates final enactment, so students should compare its reform critique with the enacted 2023 law.
