## Use It For

Use this for the prosecutorial supervision side of private-company protection, especially when criminal procedure, property freezing, cross-region enforcement, or profit-driven enforcement affects company operations.

## Teaching Notes

This source helps students see that private-enterprise protection is not only a civil-court issue. It also depends on supervision over investigation, prosecution, seizure, freezing, and execution.
