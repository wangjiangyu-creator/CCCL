## Brief English Introduction

Peng uses Guiding Case No. 10 to argue that courts should look beyond formal resolution validity when a close-company decision defeats the minority shareholder's legitimate expectations.

## Use It For

Use this with Guiding Case No. 10 and the comparative oppression cases to test when a formally valid governance act should still trigger minority-protection concerns.
