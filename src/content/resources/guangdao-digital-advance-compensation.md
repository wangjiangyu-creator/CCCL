## Brief English Introduction

Guangdao Digital illustrates advance compensation after serious disclosure misconduct at a BSE company, with the sponsoring securities firm helping fund investor recovery.

## Use It For

Use this case for BSE regulation, sponsor incentives, delisting consequences, and compensation without full individual litigation.

## Teaching Notes

Compare it with Zijing Storage to ask how advance compensation scales across different boards and issuer profiles.
