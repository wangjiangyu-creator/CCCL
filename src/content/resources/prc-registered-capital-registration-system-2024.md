## Use It For

Use this with Article 47 of the revised Company Law for contribution-period problems, old-company transition problems, and abnormal registered-capital hypotheticals.

## Teaching Notes

This is one of the most important post-revision implementing instruments. It moves capital rules from doctrine into registration administration.
