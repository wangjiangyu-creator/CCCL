## Use It For

Use this after Van Gorkom and Caremark to distinguish poor process, bad faith, and conscious disregard.

## Teaching Notes

Students should avoid using Disney as a simple plaintiff victory. Its value is in the standard it articulates and the high bar it illustrates.
