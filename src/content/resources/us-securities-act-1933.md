## Use It For

Use this with the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 to distinguish primary-market disclosure from ongoing reporting, trading-market regulation, and fraud liability.

## Teaching Notes

The statute is a useful comparator for Chinese IPO registration reform because it places disclosure and liability at the center of market entry.
