## Use It For

Use this when formation problems turn on name availability, misleading names, name declarations, or the relationship between private branding choices and the public registration system.

## Teaching Notes

Name registration is a useful entry point for showing students that incorporation is not only a private contract among founders; it is also an administrative screening and disclosure process.
