## Brief English Introduction

Kangmei is the landmark test of the Securities Law's special representative litigation mechanism and the opt-out style protection of dispersed public investors.

## Use It For

Use this case for securities class-action design, director and controller accountability, gatekeeper liability, and mass-investor compensation.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students how special representative litigation changes the settlement leverage of public investors compared with ordinary individual claims.
