## Brief English Introduction

The case is useful for distinguishing a board's collective decision from informal signatures, communications, or post hoc records.

## Use It For

Use this case when students need a concrete example of how formal organ action matters in corporate governance disputes.

## Teaching Notes

It pairs well with the revised Company Law's more flexible organ design: flexibility still needs recognizable decision procedure.
