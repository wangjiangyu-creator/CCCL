## Brief English Introduction

This note situates legal representative reform within the broader governance restructuring of the 2023 Company Law revision. It is useful because students can see that the legal representative changes are part of a wider redesign of company organs and accountability.

## Use It For

Use it as a concise English practice overview before diving into the statutory text.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students which governance changes are primarily enabling and which are primarily creditor- or stakeholder-protective.
