## Brief English Introduction

This note gives English-language orientation to the company-registration measures, making it useful before students read the Chinese rule text.

## Use It For

Use it to help students separate the Company Law question from the registration-administration question.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students to identify which registration obligations matter at formation and which become important only after later changes in capital, governance, or dissolution.
