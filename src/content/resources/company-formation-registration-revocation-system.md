## Brief English Introduction

This reading focuses on the exceptional remedy of revoking formation registration. It helps students distinguish ordinary correction of registered items from a public-law decision that can destabilize the company's legal existence.

## Use It For

Use it with identity-misuse and false-document cases.

## Teaching Notes

The central policy tension is between cleansing fraudulent registration and protecting transactional stability.
