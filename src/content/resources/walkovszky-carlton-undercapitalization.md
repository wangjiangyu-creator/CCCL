## Brief English Introduction

The case is a staple comparator for the undercapitalization problem: thin capital and enterprise fragmentation are troubling, but the majority required more than the lawful use of multiple corporations and minimum insurance.

## Use It For

Use this against Chinese debates over "significant undercapitalization" to ask when capital weakness becomes abuse of limited liability.

## Teaching Notes

The dissent gives students a creditor-protection argument, while the majority makes them confront the boundary between corporate-law doctrine and legislative insurance policy.
