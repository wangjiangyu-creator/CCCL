## Brief English Introduction

Zhang treats shareholder contribution as a legal obligation that sits between private-law debt analysis and company-law capital protection.

## Use It For

Use it for more advanced discussion of whether contribution disputes should be reasoned like contract performance disputes, company-capital disputes, or both.

## Teaching Notes

This is a helpful scholarly counterweight to purely registration-centered explanations of registered capital.
