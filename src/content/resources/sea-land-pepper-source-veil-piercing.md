## Brief English Introduction

The case shows why evidence of commingling and domination is not always enough; courts also ask what kind of fraud or injustice would result from respecting separate personality.

## Use It For

Use this when students reduce veil piercing to a checklist. It forces them to identify the legal wrong that makes limited liability abusive.

## Teaching Notes

Compare the "promote injustice" analysis with Chinese courts' emphasis on serious damage to creditors under Company Law Article 23.
