## Use It For

Use this with Re Chime, Waddington, and Chinese shareholder-remedy cases to test the boundary between personal oppression claims and wrongs suffered by the company.

## Teaching Notes

The case is a strong teaching vehicle because the same facts raise oppression, derivative-action, and director-liability questions.
