## Brief English Introduction

The note links CSRC share-reduction measures with exchange implementation and disclosure thresholds for major shareholders.

## Use It For

Use it when a shareholder-rights problem involves exit through the public market rather than a private LLC transfer.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students to compare close-company transfer restrictions with listed-company share-disposal limits.
