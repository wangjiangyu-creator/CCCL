## Brief English Introduction

The case reaches beyond the issuer and managers to a business counterparty that knowingly assisted the listed company's financial fraud.

## Use It For

Use this case for joint tort liability, gatekeeper and counterparty responsibility, and the evidentiary chain behind fabricated transactions.

## Teaching Notes

It is useful for discussing whether securities law should target only issuers and intermediaries or also commercial counterparties who help create false disclosure.
