## Brief English Introduction

This article argues that CSR is not merely symbolic. Its values can be found across the Company Law's related rules, even when the CSR clause itself is general.

## Teaching Notes

Use it to ask students to find hidden CSR effects in rules on employees, creditors, disclosure, governance, and environmental compliance.
