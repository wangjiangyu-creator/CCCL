## Use It For

Use this after negative-list analysis. A sector may be open to foreign investment, but the encouraged catalogue asks a different question: whether the project fits a state-supported industry, technology, or region.

## Teaching Notes

The 2025 catalogue is useful for distinguishing access control from investment promotion. It points students toward questions about industrial upgrading, regional development, reinvestment incentives, and project-service support.
