## Use It For

Use this as the anchor text for every topic. Students should track how the 2023 revision moves Chinese company law toward tighter capital discipline, clearer director and controller duties, more flexible governance organs, and special rules for state-invested companies.

## Teaching Notes

The course should distinguish the enacted Chinese text from English summaries. For assessed work, students should cite the official Chinese text and explain any translation choices.
