## Brief English Introduction

This article is valuable as a historical baseline. It reflects the older idea that a company's legal capacity and conduct capacity were limited by registered business scope, an approach later weakened by contract-law and Civil Code reforms.

## Use It For

Use it to show what Civil Code article 505 moved away from.

## Teaching Notes

Students should read this against Wolff's comparative article on the disappearance of ultra vires in Greater China.
