## Brief English Introduction

This article updates the close-company transfer-restriction problem. It helps students evaluate when article-based transfer controls protect the personal-trust character of a limited liability company and when they unlawfully suppress exit.

## Use It For

Use it with Guiding Case No. 96 and Wan Jiayu.

## Teaching Notes

The most useful question is whether the restriction preserves company stability while leaving a reasonable exit path.
