## Use It For

Use this with the State Council name provisions when students need the practical steps for name declaration, review, correction, and dispute handling during incorporation.

## Teaching Notes

The self-declaration mechanism is a good way to discuss the shift from ex ante approval toward registrant responsibility and later correction.
