## Brief English Introduction

The case links formation-stage commitments with later capital discipline. A shareholder who remained on the register but failed to meet contribution obligations created a governance and capital problem, forcing the court to examine whether article-based procedures could support loss of shareholder status.

## Use It For

Use it to discuss the overlap between articles, contribution acceleration, shareholder expulsion, and the public registration record.

## Teaching Notes

The case is especially useful after the 2023 Company Law because contribution deadlines and shareholder-loss mechanisms now do more practical work.
