## Use It For

Use Rule 144A to compare China's company-bond and specialized bond reforms with a U.S. model that deepens institutional debt markets by limiting the resale market to qualified institutional buyers.

## Teaching Notes

The rule is not a company-law capital rule. It is included in Unit 3 because modern corporate finance often uses securities-law exemptions and institutional resale markets to raise debt capital efficiently.
