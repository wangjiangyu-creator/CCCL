## Use It For

Pair the Civil Code with the Company Law when problems turn on separate personality, authority of representatives, apparent authority, contract enforcement, or tort liability.

## Teaching Notes

The Civil Code is not a company statute, but it supplies the private-law grammar that Chinese company-law disputes often speak.
