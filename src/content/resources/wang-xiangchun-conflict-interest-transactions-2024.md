## Brief English Introduction

Wang treats conflict transactions as a single regulatory problem rather than isolated rules. That makes the article a useful bridge between statutory text and case facts involving tunneling, side businesses, and diverted opportunities.

## Use It For

Use this for related-party transaction approval, corporate opportunity claims, competing-business restrictions, and the question whether disclosure and approval should cure a conflicted transaction.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students to compare procedural cleansing with substantive fairness. The article is useful because the revised law contains both ideas.
