## Brief English Introduction

The SPC frames substantive consolidation as exceptional. It requires serious commingling, high separation costs, and a risk to equal creditor treatment.

## Use It For

Use this case for corporate groups, enterprise personality, creditor equality, and the relationship between veil-piercing logic and bankruptcy procedure.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether the case is best understood as a bankruptcy remedy, a group-law remedy, or a procedural version of personality denial.
