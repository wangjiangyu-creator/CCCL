## Brief English Introduction

This article treats CSR as a topic governed by both company law and external legal regimes. It is useful for seeing CSR as a cross-field compliance and governance issue.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students which CSR duties belong in company law and which belong in labor, environmental, consumer, or securities law.
