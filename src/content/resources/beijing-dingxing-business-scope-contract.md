## Brief English Introduction

Beijing Dingxing applies the modern rule that exceeding registered business scope is not, by itself, a reason to invalidate a contract. The validity inquiry turns on the Civil Code's general invalidity rules and any special licensing or prohibition rules for the transaction.

## Use It For

Use it to separate business-scope registration from corporate capacity and contract invalidity.

## Teaching Notes

This case should be paired with Civil Code article 505 and the older ultra vires scholarship to show the doctrinal shift.
