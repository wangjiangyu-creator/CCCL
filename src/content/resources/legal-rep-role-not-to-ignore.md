## Brief English Introduction

This background reading presents the legal representative as a central node in the company's power system. It is especially useful for students new to Chinese company law because it explains why a registered individual can matter so much for contracts, filings, litigation, enforcement, and internal control.

## Use It For

Use it as a quick conceptual primer before moving to the more technical cases and articles.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students to list the practical risks that would not exist in the same way in a board-centered representation system.
