## Brief English Introduction

This practitioner series is useful for turning the statutory list of article contents into drafting choices about governance structure, shareholder rights, capital contributions, and representative authority.

## Use It For

Use it in seminar to ask which article provisions are mandatory, which are default rules, and which are drafting opportunities.

## Teaching Notes

Students should cross-check practitioner recommendations against the statute before treating them as legal conclusions.
