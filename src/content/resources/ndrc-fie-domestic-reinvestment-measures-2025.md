## Use It For

Use this for post-establishment foreign-investment planning, especially when an existing FIE or foreign shareholder reinvests China-source profits into another domestic company or project.

## Teaching Notes

The measures show that foreign-investment law is not only a gatekeeping regime. It also includes promotion tools, project libraries, reinvestment services, tax and foreign-exchange coordination, and information-sharing mechanisms.
