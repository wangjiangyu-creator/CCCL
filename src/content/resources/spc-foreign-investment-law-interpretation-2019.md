## Use It For

Use this when a foreign-investment contract, nominee arrangement, equity transfer, or capital contribution dispute raises validity questions after the Foreign Investment Law.

## Teaching Notes

The interpretation is especially useful for teaching the distinction between prohibited sectors, restricted sectors, and sectors outside the negative list. It pushes courts toward validity where the investment is outside the negative list or where a restricted-sector defect has been cured before judgment.
