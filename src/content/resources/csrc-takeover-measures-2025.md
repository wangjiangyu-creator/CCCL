## Use It For

Use this for listed-company control transactions, tender offers, agreement acquisitions, indirect acquisitions, disclosure thresholds, and takeover exemptions.

## Teaching Notes

This resource supersedes the 2020 amendment. Pair it with CSRC Securities and Futures Law Application Opinion No. 19 for Articles 13 and 14, especially when teaching shareholding-disclosure thresholds and concerted-action control analysis. The source link is the MOFCOM policy database's consolidated text; the amendment is cited to CSRC Order No. 227.
