## Brief English Introduction

Wang challenges the common habit of explaining limited liability only from the company's external debt relationship. The article emphasizes that shareholders' core duty is to contribute capital to the company.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students how this view changes the analysis of unpaid contributions, creditor claims, and veil piercing.
