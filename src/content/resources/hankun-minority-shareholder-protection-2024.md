## Brief English Introduction

The note is useful because it groups shareholder rights by litigation function: governance participation, economic rights, exit, and enforcement.

## Use It For

Use it as a checklist for building a shareholder dispute file under the 2023 Company Law.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students to classify each remedy as a common-interest right, an individual economic right, an exit right, or a company enforcement right.
