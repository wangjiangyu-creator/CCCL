## Brief English Introduction

Yao and Zhang frame unauthorized representative acts as a systemic problem produced by China's single legal representative structure. Their analysis asks how the legal rules should protect corporate personality while still keeping external transactions predictable.

## Use It For

Use it after the cases so students can see how individual disputes form a larger normative cluster.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether a "representative theory" account produces different results from an agency account in hard cases.
