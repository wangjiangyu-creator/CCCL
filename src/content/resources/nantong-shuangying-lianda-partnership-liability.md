## Brief English Introduction

The case asks whether the parties' actual cooperation and contribution arrangement can reveal a partnership even when the business appears externally as a different form.

## Use It For

Use this case to compare partnership liability with company limited liability, especially where registration and economic reality point in different directions.

## Teaching Notes

It is a practical bridge from "what form was registered?" to "who actually bore entrepreneurial risk?"
