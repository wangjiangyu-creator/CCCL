## Brief English Introduction

The dispute concerned whether a claimant who said he beneficially owned shares in special purpose vehicles could treat assets held by those companies as his own through reverse piercing.

## Use It For

Use it with the SPC FaDaWang Q&A on reverse personality denial to compare why courts are especially cautious when insiders ask to disregard a company.

## Teaching Notes

The case is a clean taxonomy source: standard piercing, outsider reverse piercing, and insider reverse piercing involve different plaintiffs and different risks to creditors and shareholders.
