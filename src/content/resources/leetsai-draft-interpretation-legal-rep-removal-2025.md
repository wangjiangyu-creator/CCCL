## Brief English Introduction

This note highlights the draft interpretation's proposed path for legal representatives to seek change or expungement of registration information through litigation. It also explains the transaction-security compromise: until the register changes, the company may remain bound to good-faith counterparties.

## Use It For

Use it alongside the Norton Rose note to compare practitioner readings of the same draft provisions.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students how the law should allocate loss when resignation has taken effect internally but the public register remains unchanged.
