## Brief English Introduction

The case treats company equity and company-held assets as distinct legal objects. Selling shares does not itself transfer the company's mining right.

## Use It For

Use this case for asset-versus-share sale analysis, regulatory approvals, and the property consequences of separate legal personality.

## Teaching Notes

The case is a neat transaction-structuring problem: what changes when parties sell the corporate wrapper instead of the regulated asset?
