## Brief English Introduction

This article gives a compact comparative account of business scope. It explains how ultra vires doctrine developed and why many systems now protect transaction validity despite business-scope overreach.

## Use It For

Use it to add comparative context to the PRC business-scope rules.

## Teaching Notes

Students should compare public-law business licensing with private-law contract validity.
