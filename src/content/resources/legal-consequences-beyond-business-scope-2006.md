## Brief English Introduction

This article anticipates the modern rule that exceeding business scope should not automatically decide contract validity. It treats business scope as a regulatory and disclosure device rather than the outer boundary of civil capacity.

## Use It For

Use it between older ultra vires theory and Civil Code article 505.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether regulatory illegality and civil validity should be separated.
