## Brief English Introduction

This Korean-indexed article treats unauthorized corporate guarantees as a particular form of ultra vires representation. It is useful because it organizes competing Chinese judgment paths and emphasizes review of articles and corporate resolutions.

## Use It For

Use it with Jiu Min Minutes, the company-guarantee cases, and legal-representative authority readings.

## Teaching Notes

Students should distinguish ultra vires corporate capacity from ultra vires representative authority.
