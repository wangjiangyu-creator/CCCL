## Brief English Introduction

This record anchors the basic hierarchy between statutory mandatory norms and charter autonomy. It is useful because students often overstate the phrase "company autonomy" without first asking whether the statutory rule can be displaced.

## Use It For

Use it to test articles clauses that alter voting, transfer, director, or meeting rules.

## Teaching Notes

The hard part is classifying the statutory rule: mandatory, enabling, supplementary, or default.
