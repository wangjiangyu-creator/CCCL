## Use It For

Use this as a live financing example for convertible bonds. It shows how a registration approval, offering announcement, original-shareholder priority allocation, online subscription, bond terms, and conversion mechanics appear in practice.

## Teaching Notes

Because this is a market disclosure rather than a judicial decision, use it as a transaction document. It helps students read the financing mechanics that sit behind the Company Law and CSRC convertible-bond rules.
