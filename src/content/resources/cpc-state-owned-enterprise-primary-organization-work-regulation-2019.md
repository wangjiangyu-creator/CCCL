## Use It For

Use this with Company Law Article 170 to explain Party organization leadership, prior discussion of major business and management matters, and the embedding of Party-building requirements into SOE articles and governance procedure.

## Teaching Notes

Students should distinguish Party organization pre-discussion from formal board or management decision-making. The regulation helps explain the governance sequence, while the Company Law identifies the corporate-organ framework.
