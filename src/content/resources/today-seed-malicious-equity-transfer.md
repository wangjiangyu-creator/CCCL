## Brief English Introduction

Today Seed shows that capital-contribution doctrine can appear inside an intellectual-property case when shareholders use unpaid subscribed capital and equity transfer to evade an existing debt.

## Use It For

Use it to test whether Article 88-style problems are only about ordinary commercial debts or also about tort and IP liabilities.

## Teaching Notes

The facts are vivid: zero-price transfer, a plainly weak transferee, and subsequent capital reduction. Ask which facts show bad faith.
