## Use It For

Use this as an updated companion to the 2025 mediation release. It includes a cross-border shareholder contribution dispute that the court resolved through an integrated settlement strategy.

## Teaching Notes

The release helps students see the court's current emphasis on equal protection of Chinese and foreign parties, multi-case settlement, and substantive resolution of long-running cross-border governance disputes.
