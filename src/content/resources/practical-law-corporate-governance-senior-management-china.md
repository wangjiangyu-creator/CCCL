## Brief English Introduction

This practice note gives students a current English map of Chinese LLC governance after the revised Company Law. It is useful because it places legal representatives beside shareholders, directors, supervisors, and senior management instead of treating the role in isolation.

## Use It For

Use it as a quick English reference for students preparing for governance and officer-liability units.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students to identify which parts of Chinese governance are familiar from comparative company law and which parts are institutionally distinctive.
