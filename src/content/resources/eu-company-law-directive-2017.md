## Use It For

Use Chapter IV as a comparative legal-capital reference point for minimum capital, distributions, own-share acquisitions, capital increases, and capital reductions.

## Teaching Notes

Pair the directive with the Enriques, Macey, and Armour critiques so students compare legal-capital doctrine with arguments about whether it actually protects creditors.
