## Brief English Introduction

Zhu's article is a foundational Chinese-language argument for treating controlling-shareholder power as legally constrained, not merely an incident of voting strength.

## Use It For

Use this for the normative basis of controller duties and for reading new Company Law Articles 22, 89(3), 180(3), and 192 against a longer scholarly debate.
