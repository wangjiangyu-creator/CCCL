## Brief English Introduction

The case set translates private-economy protection into ordinary private-law disputes: financing cost, good faith, limited liability, historical liabilities, enterprise reputation, and enforcement design.

## Use It For

Use this when students assume private-enterprise protection is purely administrative. The cases show it operating through contract, finance, company, and execution doctrines.
