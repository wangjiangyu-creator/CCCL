## Brief English Introduction

This article gives students the administrative-law side of formation disputes. Registration authorities normally examine whether required documents are complete and facially lawful, while excluding obvious and serious defects that can be identified within their review capacity.

## Use It For

Use it to evaluate claims that a registration authority should have detected forged signatures, seals, or false supporting documents.

## Teaching Notes

Pair it with Zhang Wen to ask how courts should remedy identity-misuse registration after the fact.
