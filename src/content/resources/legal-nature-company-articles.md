## Brief English Introduction

This reading supplies the theoretical vocabulary for the contract-versus-autonomous-rule debate. It is helpful for explaining why articles can bind non-signing directors, future shareholders, and corporate organs in ways ordinary contracts do not.

## Use It For

Use it when students ask whether articles are just a shareholder contract.
