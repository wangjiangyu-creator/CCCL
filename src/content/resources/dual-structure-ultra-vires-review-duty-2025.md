## Brief English Introduction

This reading explains the two-track structure of ultra vires representation after the Contract Book Interpretation: statutory limits and agreed limits have different effects and different review expectations. Reasonable review sits between purely formal checking and full substantive investigation.

## Use It For

Use it to make the counterparty's role concrete in guarantee and authority disputes.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students what documents a prudent counterparty should request before relying on a legal representative's signature or seal.
