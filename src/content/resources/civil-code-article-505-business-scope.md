## Brief English Introduction

Article 505 is the central modern rule on business-scope overreach. It rejects automatic invalidity and directs courts to evaluate the contract under ordinary Civil Code validity rules and any specific mandatory restrictions.

## Use It For

Use it as the statutory baseline before reading ultra vires business-scope cases.

## Teaching Notes

The provision is short, but it rewires older debates about corporate capacity and registered business scope.
