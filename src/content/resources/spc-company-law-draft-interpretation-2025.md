## Use It For

Use the shareholder-contribution chapter as the most current map of likely judicial treatment of non-monetary contributions, debt set-off, burden of proof, contribution acceleration, forfeiture, director collection duties, capital withdrawal, and illegal capital reduction.

## Teaching Notes

Because this is still a draft, students should separate settled law from proposed clarification. It is ideal for asking where courts think the 2023 capital reforms still need operational rules.
