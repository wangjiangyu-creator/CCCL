## Brief English Introduction

Jintongling shows investor damages being asserted and paid in coordination with a listed company's reorganization, rather than waiting outside the insolvency process.

## Use It For

Use this case for securities claims in bankruptcy, representative litigation, restructuring plans, and public-investor recovery.

## Teaching Notes

It forces students to ask whether securities investors are ordinary tort creditors, special capital-market claimants, or both.
