## Brief English Introduction

The case expands representative litigation to a mixed A/B-share investor base, raising questions about public investor scope and cross-market harm.

## Use It For

Use this case for shareholder classes, market segmentation, disclosure fraud, and procedural design in multi-market listed companies.

## Teaching Notes

It is a compact way to ask whether different trading boards should lead to different remedial treatment when the issuer's disclosure is common.
