## Use It For

Use this when a foreign investor's legal problem starts with sector access rather than shareholder mechanics.

## Teaching Notes

The 2024 list matters because it removed the remaining manufacturing-sector foreign-investment restrictions while preserving restrictions in other sensitive areas.
