## Brief English Introduction

This article studies the limited liability company as a specific Mainland company form. It is useful for distinguishing the general idea of limited liability from the institutional design of LLCs.

## Teaching Notes

Use it when comparing limited liability companies, companies limited by shares, one-person companies, and foreign-invested enterprise forms.
