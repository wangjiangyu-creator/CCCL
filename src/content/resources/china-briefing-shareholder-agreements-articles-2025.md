## Brief English Introduction

This article explains the draft rule that shareholder agreements normally bind only the signatory shareholders, with limited exceptions for unanimous consent, statutory authorization, or formal company ratification. It is directly useful for the articles-versus-shareholder-agreement problem.

## Use It For

Use it with the formation-agreement/articles-priority record.

## Teaching Notes

The article is also useful for asking whether confidential shareholder agreements should ever bind later shareholders.
