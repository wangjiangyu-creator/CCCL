## Brief English Introduction

Kunming Min Paper secretly discharged wastewater and caused major ecological damage. Because its shareholders mixed company and personal assets, collected company receivables through personal bank cards, and held company real estate in personal names, the court denied the company's separate personality and imposed joint liability on the shareholders for environmental restoration and appraisal costs.

## Use It For

Use this as the main case linking limited liability, environmental tort debt, creditor protection, and corporate social responsibility.

## Teaching Notes

Students should separate two questions: why the company was liable for pollution, and why shareholders were liable for the company's environmental debt. The case shows that veil piercing can protect public-interest environmental creditors, not only ordinary commercial creditors.
