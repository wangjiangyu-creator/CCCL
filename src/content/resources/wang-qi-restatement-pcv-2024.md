## Brief English Introduction

Wang argues that veil piercing now does more than restrain shareholders. It increasingly regulates abusive control by actual controllers and affiliated enterprises, while protecting creditors and deterring misconduct.

## Teaching Notes

Use it after Article 23(2) to discuss whether China's doctrine is becoming a broader control-abuse doctrine.
