## Brief English Introduction

The decision emphasizes disclosure of related-party transactions, substantive review of commercial structure and price, and disgorgement or damages where an unnecessary affiliate layer extracts value.

## Use It For

Use this with Company Law Articles 22, 182, 186, and 188, and with Interpretation V Article 1 on why formal procedure alone does not defeat a company-interest harm claim.

## Teaching Notes

The case is a good antidote to treating related-party approval as a box-checking exercise. Ask students what evidence shows an actual transfer of value.
