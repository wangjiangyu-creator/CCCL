## Brief English Introduction

Zhao offers a system-level reading of fiduciary duties under the revised Company Law, making it especially useful for problems that involve several actors at once.

## Use It For

Use it when a problem includes directors, supervisors, senior officers, controlling shareholders, and actual controllers, and students must avoid collapsing all responsibility into one generic fiduciary label.

## Teaching Notes

Pair this with the Unit 5 hypotheticals and ask students to build a responsibility matrix: actor, source of duty, breach theory, claimant, and remedy.
