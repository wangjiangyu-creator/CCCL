## Use It For

Use this to connect private-economy policy to private-company governance: improved corporate governance, shareholder discipline, internal supervision, compliance operation, risk management, and separation between company property and investor or family property.

## Teaching Notes

This is a policy source, not a Company Law provision. It is still useful because later statutes, judicial guidance, and implementation measures build on the same policy architecture.
