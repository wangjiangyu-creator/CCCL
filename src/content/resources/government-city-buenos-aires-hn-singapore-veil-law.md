## Brief English Introduction

The court treated veil lifting as a question tied to the company status created by incorporation, not simply as a contractual consequence governed by the contract's proper law.

## Use It For

Use this case when students ask which legal system should decide veil-piercing liability in cross-border group structures.

## Teaching Notes

The decision pairs well with Chinese cases involving offshore holding structures, foreign contracts, or related companies incorporated in multiple jurisdictions.
