## Use It For

Use this for equity-transfer disputes where contract remedies must be adjusted to the organizational consequences of changing shareholder status.

## Brief English Introduction

Guiding Case No. 67 holds that the special termination rule for installment sales does not automatically apply to limited liability company equity transfers. Courts must account for transfer registration, shareholder acceptance, and transactional stability.

## Teaching Notes

Pair this with nominee-shareholding and transfer-restriction materials so students see that equity contracts are not ordinary asset-sale contracts.
