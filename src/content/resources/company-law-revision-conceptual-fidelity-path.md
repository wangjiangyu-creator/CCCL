## Brief English Introduction

This source discusses the basic ideas that should remain stable during company-law reform and the paths through which reform should proceed. It is useful for seeing company-law revision as a problem of legal philosophy, not only technical drafting.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students which ideas should be stable across reforms: autonomy, creditor protection, investor protection, state policy, or sustainable development.
