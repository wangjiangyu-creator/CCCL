## Use It For

Use this with Regal and Bhullar to compare strict and factor-based approaches to corporate opportunities.

## Teaching Notes

The case is a helpful comparator for Article 183 because it asks whether the opportunity is corporate in substance, not merely whether the company had already signed a contract.
