## Brief English Introduction

This source maps the doctrinal variants of veil piercing. It is useful for showing that ordinary shareholder-to-company piercing is only one possible direction.

## Teaching Notes

Use it with the SPC's discussion of reverse piercing and with horizontal-piercing cases involving affiliated companies.
