## Brief English Introduction

The draft interpretation would make statutory organ boundaries harder to contract around. A shareholders' meeting or board resolution that exceeds legal powers may be invalid, while a resolution that only conflicts with the articles is usually handled through revocation.

## Use It For

Use this record to update the resolution-validity discussion after the 2023 Company Law.

## Teaching Notes

Because this is still a draft, students should treat it as evidence of judicial direction rather than binding law.
