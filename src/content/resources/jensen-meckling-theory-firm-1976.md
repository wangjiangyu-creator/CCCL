## Use It For

Use this as the entry point for agency theory and for explaining why company law builds monitoring, voting, fiduciary duties, disclosure, and incentive mechanisms around delegated management.

## Teaching Notes

Pair it with stakeholder and stewardship theory so students see that governance theory is not one voice.
