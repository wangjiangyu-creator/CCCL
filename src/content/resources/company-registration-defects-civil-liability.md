## Brief English Introduction

This reading treats formation registration as more than an administrative filing. It helps students see why defects in capital, shareholder identity, or articles content can affect proof, reliance, and liability without always destroying the company's existence.

## Use It For

Use it to build the conceptual map for defective formation cases.
