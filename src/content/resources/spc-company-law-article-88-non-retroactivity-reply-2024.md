## Use It For

Use this reply whenever a problem involves transfer of shares or equity with unpaid subscribed capital and the transfer date sits near 1 July 2024.

## Teaching Notes

The reply is a clean example of how transition rules can reshape capital-contribution liability even when the substantive Company Law article looks straightforward.
