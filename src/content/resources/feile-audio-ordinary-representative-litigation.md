## Brief English Introduction

The case was an early full-scale use of ordinary representative litigation after the SPC's representative-litigation rules for securities disputes.

## Use It For

Use this case for local financial-court innovation, group securities claims, loss calculation, and the practical difference between ordinary and special representative litigation.

## Teaching Notes

It fits between individual securities lawsuits and the later Kangmei special representative litigation, showing how procedure evolved in stages.
