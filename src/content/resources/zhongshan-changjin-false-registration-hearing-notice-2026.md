## Brief English Introduction

The notice is useful as a current administrative-practice example, not as a final judicial judgment. It shows the types of documents that become central in a false-registration investigation: the application form, articles, appointment materials, identity documents, lease, domicile certification, and signature evidence.

## Use It For

Use this with Company Law Article 39 and the Company Registration Administration Measures to discuss how registration authorities handle forged signatures, false identity information, and fraudulent formation filings.

## Teaching Notes

Students should be told expressly that this is a hearing notice. It is evidence of the registration authority's process and allegations, not a final court adjudication of liability.
