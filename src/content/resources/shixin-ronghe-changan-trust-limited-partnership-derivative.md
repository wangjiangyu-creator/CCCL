## Brief English Introduction

The case shows a limited partner attempting to protect partnership interests when the partnership or its executive partner does not pursue the claim.

## Use It For

Use this case for limited-partnership funds, investor enforcement, and the overlap between partnership law, trust structures, and listed-company transactions.

## Teaching Notes

It gives students a non-company version of derivative enforcement and raises the question of why different business forms need similar monitoring devices.
