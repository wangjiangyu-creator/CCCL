## Brief English Introduction

This record highlights the articles as a source of concrete governance standards. If a meeting procedure, voting method, or resolution content violates the articles, shareholders may use the resolution-revocation remedy rather than treating every defect as automatic invalidity.

## Use It For

Use it to distinguish invalid, revocable, and non-established resolutions.

## Teaching Notes

The case is best read with Company Law rules on meeting defects and Guiding Case No. 10.
