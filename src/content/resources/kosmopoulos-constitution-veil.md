## Brief English Introduction

The case shows why courts are wary of letting shareholders invoke veil piercing to escape burdens of incorporation, even when ordinary private law may still supply a remedy.

## Use It For

Use it with insider reverse-piercing materials and Macaura to ask who is asking the court to disregard personality, and whose interests would be harmed.

## Teaching Notes

The court's refusal to lift the veil is as important as the insurance-law result: the shareholder got a remedy without erasing corporate personality.
