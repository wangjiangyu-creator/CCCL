## Brief English Introduction

During enforcement, the debtor-side actors transferred equity for no consideration and changed the registered legal representative to a 99-year-old person with no realistic capacity to perform the role. The court treated the maneuver as an abuse of registration to disrupt enforcement and harm creditor interests.

## Use It For

Use it to show that legal representative changes can be scrutinized as execution-avoidance conduct, not merely as neutral registration events.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students how enforcement courts should distinguish legitimate management succession from evasive substitution.
