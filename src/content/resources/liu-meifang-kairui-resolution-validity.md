## Brief English Introduction

This case anchors the doctrinal distinction between invalid, revocable, and non-existent resolutions in a concrete company dispute.

## Use It For

Use this case for meeting procedure, voting defects, shareholder challenges, and the remedial consequences of flawed corporate acts.

## Teaching Notes

It helps students see why corporate procedure is not mere paperwork: procedure identifies whose will counts as the company's will.
