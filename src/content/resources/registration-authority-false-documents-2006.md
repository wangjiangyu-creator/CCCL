## Brief English Introduction

This article gives historical background for forged-material disputes. It asks whether a registration made on false proof documents should stand, when the authority should detect the falsity, and what liability follows.

## Use It For

Use it to connect formal review doctrine with practical fraud risks.

## Teaching Notes

Students should compare the older administrative-registration framework with the modern market-entity registration regime.
