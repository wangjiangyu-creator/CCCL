## Brief English Introduction

This reading helps students understand why a statutory permission to provide otherwise in the articles is not a blank cheque. Courts still need to identify the type of norm, the affected parties, and whether the clause undermines basic company-law structure.

## Use It For

Use it with transfer restrictions, voting thresholds, meeting methods, and other article-based variations.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether the clause protects company-specific ordering or entrenches controller power.
