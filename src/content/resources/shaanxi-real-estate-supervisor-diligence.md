## Brief English Introduction

The case treats supervision as an active monitoring role rather than a ceremonial position, especially where the supervisor has finance functions and actual knowledge of harmful transactions.

## Use It For

Use this for supervisor diligence, finance-staff overlap, failure to intervene, and the line between passive omission and participation in company-interest harm.

## Teaching Notes

Students should identify the separate sources of responsibility: formal supervisor status, finance-role knowledge, failure to correct, and participation in the transfers.
