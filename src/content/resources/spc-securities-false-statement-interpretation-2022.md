## Use It For

Use this for any Unit 8 problem involving listed-company disclosure misstatements, omissions, delayed disclosure, transaction causation, loss causation, or liability allocation among issuers, directors, controllers, and intermediaries.

## Teaching Notes

This interpretation is the litigation bridge between public-market disclosure duties and private investor compensation.
