## Brief English Introduction

Cai studies how supervisor liability actually operates, making the article a useful counterweight to purely formal descriptions of supervisory boards.

## Use It For

Use this for supervisor duties, the monitoring gap in closely held and listed companies, and the policy reasons behind allowing audit committees to replace supervisory boards in some companies.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether weak supervisor enforcement is a problem of legal design, incentives, information access, or litigation cost.
