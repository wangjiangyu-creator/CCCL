## Use It For

Use this when students need the current practical filing requirements for company formation, change registration, capital contribution dates, loss of shareholder rights, loss-covering capital reduction, migration, real-name verification, or registration-agent information.

## Teaching Notes

This is a practice-facing source rather than a doctrinal statute. It is valuable because it shows how the revised Company Law and 2025 registration measures have been translated into the forms and materials used by registration authorities from 1 May 2026.
