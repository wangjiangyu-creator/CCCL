## Brief English Introduction

This reading focuses on the special authority of the single legal representative compared with other officers, employees, or agents. The key point is that the legal representative combines public status with broad external representative power, so courts cannot analyze the person's overreach exactly like ordinary agency overreach.

## Use It For

Use it when students are tempted to treat all unauthorized company acts as the same problem.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students why the counterparty's duty to check authority should vary with the actor's corporate position.
