## Brief English Introduction

Chang criticizes views that define the company only as an independent legal person. The article emphasizes shareholders' ownership interests and treats the company as a basic investment tool.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether this shareholder-centered view is compatible with the revised Company Law's stakeholder and social-responsibility language.
