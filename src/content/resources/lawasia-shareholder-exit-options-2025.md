## Brief English Introduction

The note explains how Article 89's oppression-based repurchase mechanism may operate as a minority-shareholder exit device.

## Use It For

Use it for close-company deadlock, controller oppression, buyout, deregistration, and exit strategy hypotheticals.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students what facts show abuse of shareholder rights, severe harm, and a reasonable repurchase price.
