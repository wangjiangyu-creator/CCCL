## Brief English Introduction

This case shows judicial caution toward belated challenges to formation registration. A shareholder who used the company's formation materials for a public listing process could not, after a later private dispute, undo the registration merely by attacking the authenticity of seals.

## Use It For

Use it to discuss timing, reliance, and the evidentiary burden when insiders attack registration documents after the company has operated publicly.
