## Use It For

Use this for the distinction between contractual investment entitlement and recognition as a shareholder against the company and other shareholders.

## Brief English Introduction

The case recognizes the internal validity of a dormant-investor/nominee arrangement but treats the move from investment entitlement to registered shareholder status as functionally similar to an equity transfer requiring other-shareholder consent.

## Teaching Notes

It is a useful bridge from Interpretation III to current disputes over whether the shareholder register, commercial registration, contract, contribution, or actual exercise of rights should control.
