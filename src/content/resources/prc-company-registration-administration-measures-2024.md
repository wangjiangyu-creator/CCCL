## Use It For

Use this as the main post-2023 Company Law registration instrument when students need to identify which facts must be registered, which changes require amendment registration, and how company-law rules become public-record administration.

## Teaching Notes

Pair this with the Market Entity Registration Regulation. The regulation supplies the general market-entity framework; these measures show how that framework is operationalized specifically for companies.
