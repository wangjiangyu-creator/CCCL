## Brief English Introduction

This note explains how Article 191 gives harmed third parties a more direct path to compensation from directors and senior executives who act intentionally or with gross negligence while performing duties. It helps students separate company attribution from personal responsibility for serious misconduct.

## Use It For

Use it when discussing whether legal representative risk should remain indirect through the company or become direct to outsiders.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether Article 191 is best understood as creditor protection, tort responsibility, or a duty-of-care enforcement device.
