## Brief English Introduction

This article analyzes limited liability through economic efficiency, risk allocation, and institutional limits. It helps students see limited liability as a design choice rather than a natural rule.

## Teaching Notes

Use it together with veil-piercing materials to ask when economic benefits justify shifting risk to creditors or tort victims.
