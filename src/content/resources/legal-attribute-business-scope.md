## Brief English Introduction

This reading gives students a doctrinal argument for treating business scope as separate from general civil capacity. It supports the view that the legal effect of a company act should be assessed independently from the registration label.

## Use It For

Use it with Beijing Dingxing and Civil Code article 505.
