## Brief English Introduction

Basic is a key comparative case for materiality and reliance in securities false-statement litigation.

## Use It For

Use this with the SPC false-statement interpretation when students compare Chinese transaction-causation rules with U.S. fraud-on-the-market doctrine.

## Teaching Notes

The case is especially helpful for merger rumor and preliminary-negotiation hypotheticals.
