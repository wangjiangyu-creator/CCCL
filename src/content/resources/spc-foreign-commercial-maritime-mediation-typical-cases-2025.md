## Use It For

Use this when discussing dispute-resolution design for foreign investors, especially where litigation, mediation, business rescue, and preservation of investment expectations overlap.

## Teaching Notes

The mediation cases are useful for showing that foreign-investment protection is not limited to final judgments. Procedural design and settlement architecture can matter as much as substantive company-law rules.
