## Brief English Introduction

Hongda is the leading teaching case for the idea that a contribution period can be a core shareholder bargain, not merely an ordinary governance term in the articles.

## Use It For

Use it in hypotheticals where controlling shareholders shorten a minority shareholder's contribution period or otherwise weaponize capital calls.

## Teaching Notes

The case lets students distinguish article amendment formalities from substantive abuse of shareholder power.
