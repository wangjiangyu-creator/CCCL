## Use It For

Use this as a regional example of how the national foreign-investment framework can be implemented through free-trade-port rules.

## Teaching Notes

The regulation is helpful for discussing local variation. It does not replace the Foreign Investment Law, negative list, or security review, but it adds facilitation and service mechanisms in a free-trade-port setting.
