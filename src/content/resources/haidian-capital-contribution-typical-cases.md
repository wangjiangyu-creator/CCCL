## Brief English Introduction

Haidian Court's 2025 release gives students a current trial-level map of contribution-liability disputes after the Company Law revision. The cases show how capital promises become litigation over performance, evidence, nominee arrangements, creditor reliance, and strategic equity transfers.

## Use It For

Use this release as a practical companion to the five-year contribution rule and capital-credit readings.

## Teaching Notes

The cases are a good way to ask whether registered capital functions as disclosure, discipline, or both.
