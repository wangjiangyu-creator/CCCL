## Brief English Introduction

This record helps students distinguish constitutional governance terms from private founder arrangements. When the issue is internal company governance, the articles generally control; where the issue is a separate founder obligation that does not conflict with the articles, the formation agreement may still matter.

## Use It For

Use it for drafting exercises and for disputes where founders try to enforce pre-incorporation bargains after the company has adopted articles.

## Teaching Notes

The key question is not simply which document is later in time, but whether the term operates as an organization rule or a private contractual undertaking.
