## Brief English Introduction

The case connects capital contribution duties with foreign-invested company status and cross-border insolvency governance.

## Use It For

Use it when students ask whether a foreign investor's contribution obligation to a Chinese company is governed by Chinese law or the investor's home law.

## Teaching Notes

The case also previews later units on foreign investment and representative authority, so it works well as a cross-topic bridge.
