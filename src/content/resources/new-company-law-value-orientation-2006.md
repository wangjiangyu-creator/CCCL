## Brief English Introduction

This article reads the 2005 revision as a shift toward more flexible and competitive corporate organization while also strengthening duties and supervision. It presents the reform as balancing business efficiency with transaction safety.

## Teaching Notes

Use it as a historical comparison with the 2023 revision's renewed concern with capital discipline, control, and social responsibility.
