## Brief English Introduction

Lin's book uses a hand-collected dataset, practitioner interviews, and Chinese cases to analyze how venture-capital contracting works in China.

## Use It For

Use this for valuation-adjustment mechanisms, investor protections, and the contract/company-law tension in venture financing.

## Teaching Notes

This is best assigned selectively, especially the venture-capital investing chapter, rather than as a whole-book required reading.
