## Brief English Introduction

This practice note summarizes when courts are likely to support expungement under the revised Company Law. The key facts include non-participation in management, loss of substantive connection with the company, inability to represent the company, and the practical limits of internal governance remedies.

## Use It For

Use it as a practitioner-facing checklist before students read the more theoretical materials.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students which facts should be decisive: resignation notice, lack of control, lack of shareholding, company abnormality, or creditor risk.
