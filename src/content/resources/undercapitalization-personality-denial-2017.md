## Brief English Introduction

This article treats serious undercapitalization as a possible basis for corporate personality denial. It focuses on whether a company's capital is plainly inadequate for its operations and liabilities.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students why Chinese courts often emphasize commingling more than undercapitalization, and whether the 2023 capital rules change that emphasis.
