## Brief English Introduction

This IBA briefing gives an investor-facing overview of the new registered-capital system. It is useful for seeing how contribution timing, defective contribution liability, acceleration, and capital reduction operate as a connected capital-credit package.

## Use It For

Use it to connect formation registration with later shareholder-finance disputes.

## Teaching Notes

Students should test the briefing against the statutory text and the State Council transition provisions.
