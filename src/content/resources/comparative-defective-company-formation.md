## Brief English Introduction

This comparative reading frames defective formation as a choice between preventing defects before registration and correcting them after the company has begun legal life. That frame is useful for evaluating China's registration and revocation tools.

## Use It For

Use it with Zhang Wen and the revocation-system reading.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students which remedy best protects third parties who dealt with the apparently registered company.
