## Use It For

Use Article 20 for ultra vires representation problems involving statutory or agreed limits on a legal representative's power, counterparty knowledge, ratification, and attribution of the transaction to the company.

## Teaching Notes

This interpretation lets students connect company-law authority limits with general contract doctrine instead of treating ultra vires as a purely corporate-law rule.
