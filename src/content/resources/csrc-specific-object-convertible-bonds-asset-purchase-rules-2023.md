## Use It For

Use this when a listed company uses convertible debt, rather than only shares or cash, as consideration in an asset acquisition or restructuring.

## Teaching Notes

The rule helps students see that convertible bonds are not only refinancing instruments. In M&A they can become negotiated payment tools with debt, equity, pricing, lock-up, and investor-suitability features.
