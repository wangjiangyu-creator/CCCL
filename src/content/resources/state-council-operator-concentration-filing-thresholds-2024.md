## Use It For

Use this to decide whether an M&A transaction must be notified to the antimonopoly authority before closing.

## Teaching Notes

Students should separate threshold analysis from substantive competition analysis. Meeting a threshold asks whether filing is required; it does not by itself answer whether the transaction should be cleared, conditioned, or prohibited.
