## Use It For

Use this alongside Caremark and Van Gorkom to compare statutory enabling rules, charter and bylaw design, ultra vires limits, and judge-made fiduciary standards.

## Teaching Notes

Delaware is not a model to copy automatically; it is a pressure test for how flexible enabling statutes depend on litigation, markets, and professional practice.
