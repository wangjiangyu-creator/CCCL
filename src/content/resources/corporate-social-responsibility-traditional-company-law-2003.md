## Brief English Introduction

Chen explains CSR as a challenge to the idea that companies exist only to make profits for shareholders. The article also warns that Chinese legislation should not abandon the company's profit-making function too quickly.

## Teaching Notes

Use it to stage the classic CSR debate: correction to shareholder primacy or threat to company-law certainty?
