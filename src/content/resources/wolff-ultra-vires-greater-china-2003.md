## Brief English Introduction

Wolff gives students a comparative vocabulary for distinguishing corporate capacity, internal authority, business scope, and third-party protection.

## Use It For

Use it when comparing the PRC's business-scope and legal-representative questions with Hong Kong and other Greater China approaches to ultra vires.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether the doctrine truly disappears, or whether it reappears through authority, disclosure, and good-faith-review rules.
