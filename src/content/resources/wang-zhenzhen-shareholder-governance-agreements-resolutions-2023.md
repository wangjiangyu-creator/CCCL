## Brief English Introduction

Wang distinguishes shareholder governance agreements from ordinary shareholder contracts and asks how agreement-based governance interacts with formal shareholders' meeting resolutions.

## Use It For

Use this for post-2023 Company Law questions about whether shareholder agreements can structure governance and when they should affect the company or non-signatories.
