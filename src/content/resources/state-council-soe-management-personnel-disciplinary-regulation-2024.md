## Use It For

Use this for SOE manager discipline, personnel supervision, coordination with public-official discipline, and the accountability layer that sits alongside Company Law director and senior-manager duties.

## Teaching Notes

The regulation is especially useful when students ask whether a governance failure creates only civil company-law consequences. In SOEs, a single decision may also trigger internal discipline, supervisory investigation, or public-law accountability.
