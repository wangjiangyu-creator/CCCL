## Brief English Introduction

The article focuses on Company Law Article 86 and the statement that an assignee may assert shareholder rights against the company from entry in the shareholder register.

## Use It For

Use this for disputes over shareholder status, register correction, equity-transfer effectiveness, and the evidentiary relationship among shareholder registers, contribution certificates, articles, and registration records.

## Teaching Notes

The key teaching contrast is proof versus creation: does the register create shareholder status, prove it, or regulate assertion of rights against the company?
