## Use It For

Use this whenever an M&A transaction, joint venture, asset acquisition, equity acquisition, or merger may affect market competition or require concentration review.

## Teaching Notes

Merger control is a separate closing condition from company approval. A transaction can be valid under company law but still require antimonopoly filing, review, remedies, or standstill discipline.
