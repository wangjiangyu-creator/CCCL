## Brief English Introduction

This practitioner update gives a concise implementation view of the new contribution timetable. It is useful for students who need to translate the statutory five-year rule into articles drafting, investor commitments, and transition planning.

## Use It For

Use it with capital-contribution timeline exercises and formation drafting.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students how much of the capital timetable belongs in the articles, the register, and private investment documents.
