## Use It For

Use this as the English-language reference work for the basic structure and policy setting of Chinese company law, including controller-centered governance problems.

## Teaching Notes

For Unit 1, focus on the chapters on business forms, separate personality, and limited liability. For Unit 5, use the governance and controlling-shareholder discussion as background for Articles 180 and 192.
