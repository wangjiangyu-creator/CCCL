## Brief English Introduction

This book studies why limited liability evolved, how its supporting rules developed, and what value tradeoffs it creates. It is useful for deeper research projects.

## Teaching Notes

Use it for students who want to connect limited liability with theories of corporate personality and the shareholder/company interest debate.
