## Use It For

Use this with Regal and Guth to test how far a director's duty to avoid conflict extends when the company has not formally considered the opportunity.

## Teaching Notes

The facts are a good prompt for asking whether "the company was not interested" is a defense or a conclusion that itself depends on proper disclosure and board choice.
