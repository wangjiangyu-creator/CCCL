## Brief English Introduction

Wang places Chinese company-law reform in the setting of global economic competition. The article emphasizes lowering business costs and risks while treating companies as private market actors.

## Teaching Notes

Use this to ask whether competitiveness is a legislative purpose in its own right, or a means to protect investors, creditors, and market order.
