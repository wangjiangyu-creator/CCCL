## Brief English Introduction

Yao was registered as the company's legal representative because the real boss could not register a company due to credit restrictions. Evidence later confirmed that Xu was the actual controller, shifting the wage-arrears inquiry away from the nominal representative and toward the person exercising real control.

## Use It For

Use it to connect company-law registration with labor enforcement and actual-controller analysis.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether employee-creditor protection should use the same evidential tests as ordinary commercial creditor disputes.
