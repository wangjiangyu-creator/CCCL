## Brief English Introduction

Wang and Xu show why registration simplification can create fraud risk when the authority relies heavily on applicant commitments. The article is useful for balancing administrative efficiency against trust in the public register.

## Use It For

Use it with formal-review materials and identity-misuse cases.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether more substantive review would prevent fraud or simply slow ordinary registration.
