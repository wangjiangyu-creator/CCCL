## Brief English Introduction

Liu frames control as a private right with public governance consequences and argues that abuse of control should support compensation for harmed minority shareholders in appropriate cases.

## Use It For

Use this for the boundary between shareholder direct actions, derivative actions, and control-abuse compensation.
