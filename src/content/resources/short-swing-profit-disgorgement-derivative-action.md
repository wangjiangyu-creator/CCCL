## Brief English Introduction

The case treats short-swing profit disgorgement as a listed-company asset that can be pursued through a shareholder derivative action when the company does not act.

## Use It For

Use this case for Securities Law disgorgement, derivative standing, controller duties, and investor-protection institutions as governance monitors.

## Teaching Notes

It is one of the cleanest links between securities trading rules and company-law derivative enforcement.
