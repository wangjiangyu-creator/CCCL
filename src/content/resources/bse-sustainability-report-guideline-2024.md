## Use It For

Use this with the SSE and SZSE sustainability-reporting guidelines to show that CSR and ESG obligations now operate through exchange self-regulation as well as general company-law principles.

## Teaching Notes

For Unit 1, the point is conceptual: CSR has moved from a broad statutory value into reporting systems. Unit 8 addresses disclosure mechanics and securities-law consequences in detail.
