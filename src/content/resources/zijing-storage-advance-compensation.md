## Brief English Introduction

The case shows advance compensation as a front-end investor remedy when fraud has already undermined public market confidence.

## Use It For

Use this case for gatekeeper incentives, non-litigation compensation, and the relationship between administrative penalties and private recovery.

## Teaching Notes

Ask whether advance compensation is a substitute for litigation, a settlement technology, or a regulatory pressure device.
