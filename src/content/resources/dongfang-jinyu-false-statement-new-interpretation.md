## Brief English Introduction

The case applies the updated false-statement framework to materiality, transaction causation, loss causation, and the priority of controller responsibility.

## Use It For

Use this case when students need to move from administrative disclosure violations to civil liability architecture.

## Teaching Notes

The case is especially useful for asking what "追首恶" means in civil securities litigation.
