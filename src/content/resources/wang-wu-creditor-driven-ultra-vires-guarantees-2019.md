## Brief English Introduction

The article treats external guarantees as a governance problem, not merely a signature problem. It asks how creditor review can deter controllers and legal representatives from bypassing internal approval.

## Use It For

Use it after the Gazette case or Jiu Min Minutes to move from rule application to policy analysis.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether review duties should differ for banks, trade creditors, shareholders, and other counterparties.
