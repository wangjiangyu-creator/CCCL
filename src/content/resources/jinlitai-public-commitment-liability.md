## Brief English Introduction

Jinlitai is useful for teaching that public commitments by directors and senior managers are part of the securities disclosure ecosystem, not casual investor-relations language.

## Use It For

Use this case with Securities Law Article 84 and the false-statement interpretation to analyze when failure to perform a public commitment becomes investor-facing civil liability.

## Teaching Notes

The public report should be cited cautiously because the full judgment is not linked in the course library. The reliable teaching point is the liability theory: an unexecutable public commitment, repeated misleading follow-up, and serious investor reliance can create securities tort exposure for the committing officers.
