## Brief English Introduction

This article examines how ESG concerns can be embedded in directors' duties under the revised Company Law. It is useful for moving from CSR principles to board-level obligations.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether an ESG turn expands directors' discretion, increases liability, or both.
