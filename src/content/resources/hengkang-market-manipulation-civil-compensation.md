## Brief English Introduction

The case shows that market manipulation can generate private compensation, not only administrative penalties or criminal sanctions.

## Use It For

Use this case for causation, loss calculation, investor standing, and the civil side of market-abuse enforcement.

## Teaching Notes

It helps students compare disclosure-based false-statement claims with trading-based manipulation claims.
