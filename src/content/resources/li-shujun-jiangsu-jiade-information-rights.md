## Use It For

Use this as the classic information-rights case before moving to the 2023 Company Law's express inclusion of accounting vouchers and subsidiary materials.

## Brief English Introduction

The case treats accounting books and vouchers as central to meaningful shareholder inspection. A company cannot refuse inspection merely by speculating that the shareholder has another dispute-related motive.

## Teaching Notes

This is a good case for asking students what evidence a company should need before invoking the improper-purpose defense.
