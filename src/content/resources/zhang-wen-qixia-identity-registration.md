## Brief English Introduction

Zhang Wen frames identity theft in company registration as a problem requiring calibrated administrative relief. Even where the registration authority should correct a serious error, the court warned against using an administrative judgment to erase corporate personality more broadly than necessary.

## Use It For

Use this case to separate correction of false registered particulars from dissolution, invalidity, or denial of the company's civil-subject status.

## Teaching Notes

Pair it with the formal-review readings to test how far a registration authority's review duty should extend.
