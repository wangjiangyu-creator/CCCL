## Brief English Introduction

This reading focuses on when and how articles take effect. It is useful for linking initial formation, amendment, binding force, and the public-registration function.

## Use It For

Use it with Wan Jiayu and article-amendment hypotheticals.

## Teaching Notes

Students should separate internal effect from opposability against third parties.
