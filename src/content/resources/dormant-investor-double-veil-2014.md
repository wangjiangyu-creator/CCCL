## Brief English Introduction

This article considers hidden or dormant investors and asks when courts should look through both formal shareholding and corporate personality. It is useful for ownership structures that conceal control.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students to identify whether the relevant veil is the company's personality, the nominee shareholder arrangement, or both.
