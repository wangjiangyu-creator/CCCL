## Brief English Introduction

The Wuyang Bonds case extends representative litigation beyond shares into company-bond investor protection and intermediary responsibility.

## Use It For

Use this case for bondholder protection, securities issuance liability, underwriter and auditor duties, and creditor-facing disclosure.

## Teaching Notes

It is a good way to ask whether bond investors should be treated more like contract creditors or public securities investors.
