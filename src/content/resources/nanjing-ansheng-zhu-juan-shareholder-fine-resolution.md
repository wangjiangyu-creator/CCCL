## Use It For

Use this for article-based governance controls, shareholder sanctions, and the limits of majority power in a close company.

## Brief English Introduction

The case accepts that articles may discipline shareholder conduct in some circumstances, but insists that penalty standards be clear enough to avoid arbitrary majority punishment.

## Teaching Notes

Pair this with Guiding Case No. 96 to compare legitimate "people-connected" close-company arrangements with oppressive or under-specified exit and penalty mechanisms.
