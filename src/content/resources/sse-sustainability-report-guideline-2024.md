Use this for ESG and sustainability disclosure questions involving Shanghai-listed companies, especially where non-financial reporting becomes part of board and disclosure governance.

The guideline is a useful Chinese-law counterpart to OECD and UK/HK governance materials on sustainability and board accountability.
