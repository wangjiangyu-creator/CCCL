## Brief English Introduction

The claimants sought to enforce U.S. asbestos judgments against the English parent company of a group. The Court of Appeal declined to treat the parent and subsidiaries as one merely because the group structure limited liability.

## Use It For

Use this after Salomon and before Prest to show the restrictive English approach to group-company veil piercing.
