## Use It For

Use this for the trading-market side of convertible bonds, especially when students ask how a registered convertible-bond issuance becomes a listed and traded instrument.

## Teaching Notes

The exchange rules supplement the CSRC convertible-bond measures. They are useful for showing that issuance approval or registration is only the first step; market trading rules then manage price movement, liquidity, and investor protection.
