## Use It For

Use this as the mandatory due-diligence comparator for CSR and ESG. It helps students distinguish general social-responsibility clauses from operational duties to identify, prevent, mitigate, and remedy adverse impacts.

## Teaching Notes

As amended, the directive applies to a narrower set of very large companies. Students should check consolidated EU texts before relying on precise thresholds.
