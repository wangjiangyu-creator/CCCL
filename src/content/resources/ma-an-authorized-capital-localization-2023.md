## Use It For

Use this for the theoretical and comparative background to Company Law Article 152. It is especially helpful for asking whether China's reform is a full authorized-capital system or a limited localized version.

## Teaching Notes

Pair it with Delaware, UK, German, and EU materials so students can see the safety-versus-efficiency tradeoff behind capital formation rules.
