## Use It For

Use this for the 2025 policy turn toward a bond-market "technology board" and for questions about how debt-finance rules can be used to support national innovation policy.

## Teaching Notes

The announcement is useful because it links corporate debt finance to issuer expansion, flexible bond terms, simplified disclosure, specialized credit ratings, market-making, and credit-risk mitigation.
