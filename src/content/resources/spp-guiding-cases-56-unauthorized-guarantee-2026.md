## Brief English Introduction

This English release is valuable for the legal representative unit because it shows how prosecutorial supervision treats unauthorized guarantees. A counterparty that fails to verify whether the legal representative had authority to guarantee company debt cannot easily claim good faith.

## Use It For

Use it to connect legal representative authority with guarantee practice and the counterparty's reasonable-review duty.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether the company's own fault in seal management should affect liability even when the guarantee is invalid.
