## Use It For

Use this with the Civil Code and market-entity registration rules when students compare companies with individual business forms.

## Teaching Notes

The regulation is not a company statute. Its teaching value lies in showing that Chinese law recognizes several registered market-entity forms, only some of which have corporate personality and shareholder limited liability.
