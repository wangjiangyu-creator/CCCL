## Use It For

Use this for share-capital, two-tier board, and shareholder-reserved-power comparisons. For Unit 7, use sections 15-19, 291-293, 308-317, and 319-322 as a comparative model for regulating corporate groups through organization-specific rules rather than only through ex post veil piercing.

## Teaching Notes

German law helps students ask whether Chinese Article 23 is doing the work of a group-law regime, a creditor-remedy rule, or both.
