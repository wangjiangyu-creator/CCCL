## Brief English Introduction

This Chinese source treats the nature of the company as the foundation of company-law theory. It asks how different understandings of the company shape the design and boundaries of the company-law system.

## Teaching Notes

Use it to ask why abstract theories of the company matter for concrete rules on liability, governance, and corporate purpose.
