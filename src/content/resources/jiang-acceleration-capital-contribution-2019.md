## Brief English Introduction

Jiang argues that contribution deadlines are not purely private autonomy when they impair company solvency and creditor recovery.

## Use It For

Use it to contrast the older debate on non-bankruptcy acceleration with the 2023 Company Law's express contribution-acceleration rule.

## Teaching Notes

Ask students whether acceleration is best explained by company law, contract law, creditor protection, or abuse-of-right doctrine.
