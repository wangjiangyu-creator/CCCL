## Brief English Introduction

Gao and Fan criticize the older method of deciding unauthorized guarantees by classifying Company Law article 16 as an effectiveness or management mandatory norm. They argue that courts should first ask whether the legal representative's act is attributable to the company.

## Use It For

Use it to deepen the guarantee discussion after students know the basic ultra vires business-scope rule.

## Teaching Notes

The article is a strong bridge between legal-representative authority and creditor review duties.
