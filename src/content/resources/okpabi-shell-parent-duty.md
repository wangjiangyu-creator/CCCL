## Brief English Introduction

Okpabi continues the Vedanta line by treating parent liability as an ordinary negligence question based on control, intervention, supervision, advice, or assumption of responsibility.

## Use It For

Use it to separate veil-piercing doctrine from direct parent-company tort liability in corporate groups.

## Teaching Notes

The case helps students avoid a common shortcut: every group-liability problem is not necessarily a veil-piercing problem.
