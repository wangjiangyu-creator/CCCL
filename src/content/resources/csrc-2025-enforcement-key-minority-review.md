## Brief English Introduction

The CSRC review shows the enforcement background for listed-company "key minority" accountability after the revised Company Law and securities enforcement reforms.

## Use It For

Use this for current regulatory context on controller tunneling, financial fraud, administrative penalties, market bans, criminal referrals, and the interaction between public enforcement and private investor protection.

## Teaching Notes

The review reports 32 major controller-abuse or tunneling cases over RMB 100 million in 2025, including Dongxu and Guangdao examples. It should be used as regulatory context rather than as a substitute for individual penalty decisions.
