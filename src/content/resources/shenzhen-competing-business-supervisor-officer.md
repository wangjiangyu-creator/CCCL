## Brief English Introduction

The case turns a common loyalty-duty fact pattern into a concrete Chinese law problem: managers and supervisors participate in a competing entity while still owing duties to the original company.

## Use It For

Use this case for Article 180-style loyalty and diligence analysis, corporate opportunities, competing business, disgorgement, and supervisor responsibility beyond passive oversight.

## Teaching Notes

Have students identify each defendant's office, factual role, and remedy exposure. The facts help prevent a flat analysis that treats directors, officers, and supervisors as interchangeable.
