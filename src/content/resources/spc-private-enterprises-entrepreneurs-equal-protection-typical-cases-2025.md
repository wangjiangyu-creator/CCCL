## Brief English Introduction

This case set shows the court system using private-enterprise protection as a rule-of-law frame rather than a policy slogan. It covers criminal restraint, property separation, reputation, government payment, supply-chain payment clauses, innovation, and private enterprises operating abroad.

## Use It For

Use this when teaching how private-company protection cuts across criminal, civil, intellectual-property, reputation, contract, and cross-border sales disputes.
